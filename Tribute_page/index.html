A.P.J Abdul Kalam

1931 - 2015

About

Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. Known as the "Missile Man of India", he played a key role in India's space and missile programs. He was loved by students and inspired millions with his vision, humility, and dedication to the nation.

Life Timeline

  • 1931 - Born in Rameswaram, Tamil Nadu
  • 1960 - Joined DRDO as a scientist
  • 1969 - Transferred to ISRO
  • 1998 - Played key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • 2002 - Became 11th President of India
  • 2015 - Passed away while delivering a lecture
